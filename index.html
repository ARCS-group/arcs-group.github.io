The Artificial Intelligence for Robot Coordination at Scale (ARCS) Lab at the Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) Robotics Institute brings together researchers to address the challenges of multi-robot collaboration. Our research focuses on developing planning and learning methods for large teams of autonomous agents to accomplish collaborative tasks intelligently in dynamic environments.
